Co-Cirricullum Activities

Children are engaged in various co-cirricullum activities like swimming, football, music dance and drama,art and craft and many others. This applies to all children from baby class to primary six. We have acquired a 2.5 acre piece of land just opposite the school which the children use for physical education.

Academics

The school has classes from year one(babay class) upto primary six. By next year's start, it shall have upto primary seven.
We provide all necessary educational tools for children. We have a fully equiped computer room with 40 computers, children are taken for various trips respective of their classes. We also give them three sets of exams per term; Beginning of Term, Middle of Term and End of Term all in an effort to strengthen their academics. We have also introduced a boarding section.



Boarding Section

We introduced a boarding section this year and have so far registered twenty students.We intend to expand especially for upper classes inorder to help the children improve on their academics and many other activities.

Staff

Our teachers are appointed on qualifying with the critere we use to interview them. They are well qualifed and well trained to teach the students the way we want them to.
