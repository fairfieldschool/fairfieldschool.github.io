Fairfield School began as a Kindergarten in 2009.
Due to parent's demand, the primary section was later introduced.
It has progressed to primary six as per now and we shall have primary seven as well next year 2017.

Both the teaching and non-teaching staff co-operate together with an aim of achieving the best for the school.Children are served a balanced diet and they enjoy meals.The major aims of the school include high discpline, self esteem, confidence and academic excellency. We acknowledge the pieces of advice from stake holders especially our beloved parents.The school transports children both in the morning and evening at a cost which depends on the distance covered. Due to the increase in the number of children, the school purchased the 4th van to improve the transport system. We have started a boarding section although we intend to expand especially for upper classes in order to help the children improve on their academics and many other activities. The school has been able to fully open up the computer labaratory where the computer lessons for both theories and practicals will be held. There is a great emphasis on co-curricular activities like sports, art and creft music and drama amongst the others. We therefore encorage the public to buy the art items we therefore encourage the public to buy the art items made by the children under the guidance of their teachers.
